Notable anomalies

Community (14.5%) — the largest confirmed category by far, dwarfing mosque, pub, and residential. Churches become community centres far more often than the headlines suggest.

70.4% unknown — OSM records the building as a former church but no current use is tagged. These are your biggest data enrichment opportunity via planning portal cross-referencing.

814 schools in NHLE names — listed buildings called "church school" or "old school chapel" massively outnumber pub/mosque/residential conversions combined. Historical reuse as schools predates modern conversion patterns.

Geographic bias — mean centre near Warwick confirms heavy concentration in the English Midlands and South. Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland are underrepresented (OSM coverage varies; no NHLE equivalent).

Only 8 conversion years known — temporal analysis is currently almost impossible. Planning portal data will fix this — applications are dated to the day.
